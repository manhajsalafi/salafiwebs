بِسْمِ اللهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيْمِ
“ There is no fault (blame) upon the one who proclaims the madhhab of the Salaf, who ascribes to it and affiliates himself to it. Rather, it is obligatory to accept that from him by unanimous agreement, for the madhhab of the Salaf is never but the truth.”
— Ibn Taymiyyah (Majmoo' al-Fatawaa 4/149)
